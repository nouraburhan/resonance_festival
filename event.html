Awaken the Spirit of Rock

At the heart of the Resonance Festival lies a passion for bringing generations together through music. This year, we're curating a transcendent experience that not only celebrates the timeless legacy of rock but also the contemporary echoes of its influence. With the legendary Led Zeppelin taking the stage and Montreal's very own pride, Arcade Fire, this festival promises a day filled with electric performances, interactive exhibitions, and the enduring spirit of rock.

Featured Acts

Led Zeppelin

Led Zeppelin was an English rock band formed in London in 1968. The group consisted of vocalist Robert Plant, guitarist Jimmy Page, bassist/keyboardist John Paul Jones, and drummer John Bonham. They are renowned as one of the greatest rock and roll bands of all time.
